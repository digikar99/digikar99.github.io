I am Shubhamkar Ayare. I'm a doctoral student in Cognitive Science, and also an open source developer focusing on Common Lisp.

As a doctoral student, I'm pursuing my PhD at Central European University in Vienna, Austria. Under Prof. Jonathan Kominsky, my doctoral studies are motivated by Causal Representation Learning with links to Causal Selection Judgments and Theories of Actual Causation.
