* 2024-present

I'm a PhD student at the [[https://cognitivescience.ceu.edu/][Cognitive Science department]] of [[https://ceu.edu/][Central European University]] in Vienna, Austria. I am working under [[https://www.jfkominsky.com/index.html][Prof. Jonathan Kominsky]] as my primary supervisor as part of the [[https://ccl.ceu.edu][Causal Cognition Lab]]. I am also advised by [[https://people.ceu.edu/gergely_csibra][Prof. Gergely Csibra]] and [[https://people.ceu.edu/jozsef_fiser][Prof. Jozsef Fiser]] and I can usually be spotted at the Lab Meetings of the [[https://cdc.ceu.edu][Cognitive Development Center]] or the [[https://visionlab-ceu.org/][Vision Lab]]. My doctoral studies are motivated by Causal Representation Learning with links to Causal Selection Judgments and Theories of Actual Causation.

* 2021-24

Before CEU, I completed my Master's in [[https://www.cgs.iitk.ac.in/][Cognitive Science]] from [[https://www.iitk.ac.in/][IIT Kanpur]] under [[https://www.cse.iitk.ac.in/users/nsrivast/][Prof. Nisheeth Srivastava]] as part of the [[https://www.cgs.iitk.ac.in/user/nsrivast/platipus-lab/][PLATIPUS Lab]]. Besides him, I have very much enjoyed my interactions with [[https://sites.google.com/view/devpriya-kumar/home][Prof. Devpriya Kumar]] and will be forever grateful for the often lengthy (but very interesting!) discussions with [[https://sites.google.com/site/ammuns68][Prof. Narayanan Srinivasan]] amongst others.
